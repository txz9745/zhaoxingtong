Projects

Zhao Xingtong's research focuses on covert communication, with an emphasis on the application of Random Frequency Diverse Arrays (RFDA). Most existing works on covert communications using RFDA allocate the entire available bandwidth to the array in order to enhance signal strength at the target location. However, this approach may not be optimal in scenarios where the available bandwidth is large or the required level of covertness is relatively low. His current project explores more flexible and efficient bandwidth allocation strategies to improve covert communication performance under such conditions.
